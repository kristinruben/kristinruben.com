Welcome

My name is Kristin Ruben and I'm a knitter, web developer, and cat enthusiast in Cambridge, MA.

About Me

I'm an alumna of Wellesley College, where I studied geosciences and worked at the computing help desk. A former All-American swimmer, I was injured partway through college and turned to political activism.

Since then, I've worked for the U.S. Senate Committee on Environment and Public Works and for a U.S. Senate reelection campaign. After freelancing for a few years, I decided to switch careers and become a developer. I now work at Workbar in Boston.

In my spare time, I like to Instagram my knitting and my cats. I currently call Cambridge, MA home.
